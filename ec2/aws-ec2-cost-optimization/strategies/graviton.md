# Migrating to Graviton Instances

## Overview
AWS Graviton processors (ARM-based) deliver up to 40% better price-performance compared to x86.

## Benefits
- Lower cost per vCPU
- Energy efficiency
- Optimized for containers and microservices

## Migration Steps
1. Test workloads on Graviton-compatible AMIs.
2. Benchmark performance vs x86.
3. Roll out gradually to production.

## Savings Potential
Up to 40% cost savings.
