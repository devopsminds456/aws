# Auto Scaling Groups

## Overview
Automatically adjust EC2 capacity to maintain availability and performance.

## Steps
1. Define scaling policies (CPU, memory, request count).
2. Attach to multiple AZs.
3. Integrate with ELB for traffic distribution.

## Benefits
- Handles traffic spikes
- Improves resilience

## Risks
- Misconfigured policies may cause over-scaling or under-scaling.
