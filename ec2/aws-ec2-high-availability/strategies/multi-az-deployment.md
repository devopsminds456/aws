# Multi-AZ Deployment

## Overview
Deploy EC2 instances across multiple Availability Zones (AZs) to ensure resilience against AZ failures.

## Steps
1. Launch instances in at least two AZs.
2. Distribute traffic using Elastic Load Balancer.
3. Sync data across AZs.

## Benefits
- Fault tolerance
- Reduced downtime risk

## Risks
- Slightly higher costs due to redundancy.
